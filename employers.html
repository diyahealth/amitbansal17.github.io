DiyaHR is an AI-based, Software-as-a-Service platform that empowers HR with aggregated HRIS and health data. Our platform best serves self-insured coporations with a large number of employees. By enabling HR to predict and prevent high-dollar claims, DiyaHR will save corporations a minimum of 15% in annual healthcare costs.

How it works

1

Connect your exisiting HRIS system

2

Analyze automatically-generated reports

3

Engage employees in benefits programs

4

Contact and manage employees at the touch of a button

Capabilities

Quickly identify high health risk employees
Take preventative measures to help reduce high-cost claims
Promote wellness and education by sending individualized content and resources to employees
Empower HR with insights to drive strategic, enterprise-level change

Protecting Your Information is Our Top Priority

  • We integrate with e-mail services; no user names or passwords are stored on our system.
  • We store health information on the most secure cloud systems available: Microsoft Azure and Amazon Web Services.
  • We use an opt-in model: Records owned by users are never shared unless done so explicitly by the user.
  • We do not sell data to pharmaceutical companies, insurers, employers, or other businesses.
  • Our employees do not have access to patients’ records — not even our CEO.
  • We maintain a complete audit trail of all information access.
  • Reports are anonymized to protect employees' health
