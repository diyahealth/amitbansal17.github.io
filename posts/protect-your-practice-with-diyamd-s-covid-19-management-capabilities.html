COVID-19 Symptom Tracker

The COVID-19 pandemic has caused sharp declines in revenue for private medical practices, and has made office visits by patients risky for clinicians and their staff. Diya Health's novel technology addresses these difficulties with a threefold approach:
  • Remote screening for COVID-19 to avoid, when feasible, risky office visits.
  • Remote tracking, follow-up, and management of COVID-19 symptoms — all billable services.  Our research shows that these services can produce additional revenue of $75-$150 per patient per month with minimal staffing effort and cost.
  • Automatic capture of these services for billing.
Our goal is to assist clinicians in managing their patients' COVID-19 exposure while protecting their own safety and that of their staff. To this end, patients can complete, without cost, a COVID-19 self-assessment with DiyaMD's Symptom Tracker, which includes:
  • Date and time of possible exposure.
  • Common symptoms of COVID-19 ranked by relative importance.
  • Charts, generated automatically, that depict progression of a patient's symptoms.
