Emergency Access
Share your health diary with anyone you desire. With myDiya's sharing capabilities, your family members, friends, caregivers, and providers can have access to your vital health information.
In case of an emergency, your loved ones and care team can quickly and easily access your health information to ensure safe, quality treatment.
Instead of wasting precious time looking up which medications you may be allergic to or your health history, healthcare providers can immediately provide appropriate care.
Take control of your health information and prepare for any situation.
