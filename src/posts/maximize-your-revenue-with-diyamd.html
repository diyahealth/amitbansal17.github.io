What is DiyaMD?

DiyaMD is a budding telehealth platform that focuses on high efficiency, high throughput, and billable patient care services. Modeled after the customary physician practice workflow, DiyaMD helps physicians optimize their time to improve patient care and increase revenue.

Along with telehealth capabilities, DiyaMD provides many other benefits including:

  • Patient self-service online intake application
  • Insurance authorization alerts
  • Remote patient monitoring
  • Automatic billable activity capture
  • Adverse drug reaction risks and dosage duplication alerts
  • Automated trendlines to track vital signs and symptoms
  • Built in text & email tools for provider-patient and provider-provider communication
  • Dashboards of high-risk patients
  • Monthly activity reports
  • HIPAA Compliant
  • And more!

So, DiyaMD simplifies healthcare not only for providers, but also for billing staff, patients, and caregivers.
